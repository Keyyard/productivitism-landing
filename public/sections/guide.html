How it works

HOW IT WORKS IN 3 STEPS

A simple system for multitalented people: create your pixel RPG roles, assign habits and quests, then level up your real life without all-or-nothing pressure.

STEP 1

Create Your Characters

Build a pixel avatar for each role in your life, like The Developer, The Athlete, The Student, and The Creator. This role-based setup helps you stop mixing goals and start acting with clarity.

  • One character per major role keeps your goals separated
  • Customize each avatar with pixel art style and class identity
  • Give each character their own stat page and progress history
  • Switch context cleanly by leveling one role at a time
STEP 2

Set Habits & Quests

Assign daily rituals and goals to each character. Habits become repeatable XP loops, while quests move bigger projects forward without overwhelming your ADHD brain.

  • Use Check, Count, and Measure habits for different routines
  • Give every quest a clear owner character and difficulty score
  • Break big goals into subtasks and capture ideas with voice memos
  • Choose one priority character daily to reduce decision paralysis
STEP 3

Level Up Your Life

Earn XP from completed habits, quests, and focus sessions, then watch your characters grow. The app rewards consistency, not perfection, so progress feels sustainable.

  • Track growth with level progression and role-based stats
  • Use difficulty-based XP to reward meaningful effort
  • Review your behavior trends to rebalance your week
  • Keep momentum with gentle feedback and no punishment loops
BONUS

Deep Focus Without Streak Anxiety

Enter the cozy focus room for calm, distraction-light sessions with ambient sounds and no shame mechanics. It is built as an ADHD productivity app that supports real energy swings.

  • Use Countdown mode for Pomodoro-style focus blocks
  • Use Endurance mode for open-ended deep work
  • Match focus sessions to active quests and life roles
  • Use ambient soundscapes to stay in flow longer
