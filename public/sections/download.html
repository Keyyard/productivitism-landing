Frequently Asked Questions

Is Productivitism free?

Yes. Productivitism is free during Early Access on iOS.

How is Productivitism different from Habitica?

Productivitism uses a multi-character system for different life roles, avoids punishment mechanics, and adds deep pixel art customization so progress feels fun and personal.

Does Productivitism work for ADHD?

Yes. It includes an ADHD-friendly cozy focus room with ambient sounds, role-based planning, and no streak anxiety loops.

Can I use it for fitness goals?

Yes. Create a fitness character and assign workout habits, quest goals, and focus sessions to that role.

Is there an Android version?

iOS is first. Android is planned.
