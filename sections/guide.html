How it works

YOUR PLAYBOOK

Get the most out of Productivitism with these patterns — from building micro-habits to structuring complex projects.

CUSTOMIZE

Unlock Cosmetics by Playing

Every habit you check, every task you finish, every focus session you complete — they all count toward unlocking new outfits, hair styles, and accessories for your character.

  • Log 10 habits to unlock the Cap & Undercut
  • Focus for 60 min total to unlock the Chain
  • Complete 20 tasks to unlock the Cross Chain
  • 100 habit logs unlocks the Gem Chain — rarest cosmetic
HABITS

Stack Small Habits Daily

The most effective approach is many small habits rather than a few big ones. Each check-in earns XP and builds a streak that compounds over weeks.

  • Use Check type for binary habits (workout, meditate)
  • Use Count for reps (pushups, pomodoros)
  • Use Measure for tracked amounts (water ml, km run)
  • Set a weekly frequency target — 3×/week beats all-or-nothing
TASKS

Break Big Goals into Steps

Add a task for each goal, then break it down using subtasks. Set a difficulty rating — harder tasks earn more XP when completed.

  • One task per goal — subtasks for each action step
  • Difficulty 7–10 gives the biggest XP reward
  • Set due dates to trigger overdue warnings
  • Attach voice memos to capture ideas as they come
FOCUS

Use Focus to Enter Flow State

Launch a focus session before any deep work. Your character sits in the study room with you — every minute logged earns XP and counts toward cosmetic unlocks.

  • Pick Countdown for timed sessions (Pomodoro-style)
  • Pick Endurance to log open-ended deep work
  • Set a goal — "Study Chemistry", "Finish UI design"
  • Use ambient sounds to minimize distraction
